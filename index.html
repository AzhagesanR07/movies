Dear Comrade (2019) — Movie Details Language & Genre: Telugu-language romantic action drama, also released in dubbed versions (Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, Hindi)

Director & Production: Directed and written (story & screenplay) by Bharat Kamma; dialogues by Jayakrishna. Produced by Mythri Movie Makers and Big Ben Cinemas

Main Cast: Vijay Deverakonda as Chaitanya Krishna “Bobby” Makineni Rashmika Mandanna as Aparna “Lilly” Devi Shruti Ramachandran as Jaya (Lilly’s cousin, marking her Telugu debut) Wikipedia . Technical Crew: Music by Justin Prabhakaran, cinematography by Sujith Sarang, editing by Sreejith Sarang Wikipedia . Runtime & Release: Approximately 170 minutes (about 2 hours 50 minutes). Initially planned for 31 May 2019, it was released on 26 July 2019 across all language versions Wikipedia +1 . The Hindi dubbed version was later released on YouTube on 19 January 2020 by Goldmines Telefilms
